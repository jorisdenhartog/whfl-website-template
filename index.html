About Villa Aurora

Villa Aurora is a privately owned luxury holiday home located in Levi, Finnish Lapland. The villa offers spacious accommodation, Scandinavian interiors, a private Finnish sauna and everything needed for an unforgettable stay in one of Europe's most beautiful Arctic destinations. Whether you visit for skiing, hiking, mountain biking, the Northern Lights or the Midnight Sun, Villa Aurora provides a peaceful and comfortable base throughout the year.
